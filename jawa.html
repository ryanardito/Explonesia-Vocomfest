Popular Culinary

The best culinary for you

Bakso Malang

This food is made from chicken or cow meat that is formed then added sauce that has been given spices.

Gado - Gado

This food is quite healthy, the ingredients consist of boiled vegetables, tofu, tempeh, eggs, potatoes and sometimes added ketupat.

Gudeg

Gudeg is made from young unripe jack fruit stewed for several hours with palm sugar, and coconut milk.

Soto Ayam

Is a yellow spicy chicken soup with lontong or nasi himpit or ketupat and/or vermicelli or noodles.

Rujak Cingur

Seasonings consist of petis, brown sugar, chili and also peanuts that have been fried then mixed seasonings.

Kerak Telor

Is a Betawi traditional spicy omelette dish in Indonesian cuisine. It is made from glutinous rice cooked with egg.
