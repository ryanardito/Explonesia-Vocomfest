Popular Culinary

The best culinary for you

Ayam Betutu

This food is made of whole chicken or duck containing seasonings, then roasted in a chaff's fire.

Bebek Bengil

This food is made of whole duck that contains the seasoning, then fried until dry to make it crispy.

Sate Lilit

This satay is made from minced pork, fish, chicken, beef which is then mixed with grated coconut lemon juice, shallots, and pepper

Rujak Buah

This food is based on fresh fruits which are then added spice salad that has been mixed with sauce pindang.

Nasi Campur

This food is the same as rice in general but added some side dishes such as tempeh, eggs, chicken.

Nasi Be Guling

This food is the same as rice in general but added a few extra side dishes of babi guling and extra.
